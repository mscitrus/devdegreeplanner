To rearrange the degree plan, click and drag the courses into the order you desire. Courses will turn yellow if they are moved into a semester prior to their required prerequisites. Courses will turn red if they are moved to a semester that does not match the course rotation.

Disclaimer: This is a tool to help schedule your degree. It is not foolproof (particularly in alpha stage). Course rotations may change. Please verify your plan with an advisor once completed.

How courses are sorted

  • If a course is offered only in odd/even years, it receives +1 to its ranking.
  • A course receives +1 to its ranking each time it serves as a prereq for another course on the plan. For example, EPSY 6010 is required for EPSY 6005 and EPSY 6240. Therefore, it receives +2.
  • If a course is marked as high priority manually, it receives +5.
